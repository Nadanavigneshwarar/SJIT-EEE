ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT


We started our journey in August 2011. We provide world class quality of education and individual attention. The department is strived by well qualified and experienced faculty members. The department is well equipped with industry collaborated laboratories and infrastructure. All the eligible students are getting placement regularly every year in reputed companies both in core and IT sector. Most of our students are securing valid score in GRE, TOFEL, ILETS, Duo lingo, CAT, GATE to pursue higher studies in India and Abroad.
